Artist Background

Hello there! My name is Caroline Gill Rodriguez and I am a student at Nova, fully immersed in the exciting world of fine arts and graphic design. As I navigate through my academic journey, I'm passionate about exploring the diverse realms of creativity and visual expression. Beyond the confines of textbooks and lectures, you'll often find me in my element, armed with a paintbrush and a blank canvas, translating my thoughts and emotions into captivating works of art. My artistic journey began when I was just 12 years old, a time when curiosity led me to start self-teaching. From sketching to experimenting with various artistic mediums, I found a profound joy in the act of creation. What started as a personal exploration soon turned into a realization that art was more than just a hobby.
